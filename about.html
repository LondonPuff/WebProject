About Us

Who are we?

We are a luxury Shisha delivery service operating in Central London. Currently we only deliver to postcode areas SE1, SW1, EC1, W1 and WC1.

Why us?

We use the most prestigious brands in the Shisha industry such as Fumo and Fumari. Our equipment is pleasing to the eye and our tobacco lasts 3x longer than Al-Fakher and 2x longer than Starbuzz. Plus, it tastes so much better.

Why delivery?

We think that there is no better place to enjoy shisha than in your own house, surrounded by the people you love with some good quality TV and food. Most Shisha places in London are overcrowded and unenjoyable, it's about time to try something much better!
