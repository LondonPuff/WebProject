Products

We have two types of shisha flavourings: With Nicotine (FUMARI TOBACCO) and Without Nicotine (FUMO SHISHA STONES)

Fumo Shisha Stones

Shisha stones are rocks flavoured with concentrated liquid, they provide a fresh and long-lasting taste to the Shisha and are reusable, making them environmentally friendly!

We currently offer 12 flavours:

  • Apple
  • Berry Mint
  • Black Grape
  • Blueberry
  • Exotic Mix
  • Lemon Mint
  • Mint
  • Pina Colada
  • Raspberry
  • Strawberry
  • Vanilla
  • Watermelon
You can also mix flavours! Ask our shisha expert for some advice on the best combinations.

Fumari Tobacco

Fumari Shisha Tobacco is one of the finest shisha tobacco brand in the market. Their package design ensures that the tobacco stays fresh and flavourful for a long period of time.

We currently offer 12 flavours:

  • Blueberry Muffin
  • Citrus Mint
  • Granny Smith
  • Island Papaya
  • Jasmine
  • Mint Chocolate Chill
  • Orange Cream
  • Prickly Pear
  • Red Gummy Bear
  • Spiced Chai
  • Tropical Punch
  • Tutti Frutti
Please refer to our shisha expert for advice on which flavours go with each other!
