How does it work?

  1. Have a look through our price list, make up your mind and choose the Shisha you want to have.
  2. Contact us by phone and one of our staff members will take down your delivery details as well as your personal information to verify your age.
  3. Our Shisha expert will come to your location within 30 minutes with the necessary equipment and prepare your Shisha.
  4. Before the Shisha expert leaves you to enjoy your Shisha, we require for you to pay 50% of the total fee upfront for security reasons.
  5. After the selected time has passed, our expert will return back to collect and clean everything up. That is when we ask for the other 50%.

Don't worry, we make sure to always check the financials with you on the phone first so there will be no surprises. We accept cash and card payments.


Alternatively, you can always leave us a message with your personal contact details along with your order, and one of our staff members will get in touch with you within 2-3 minutes.

Classic Shisha

Our Classic Shisha consists of Luxury Shisha Equipment from Fumo, a choice of Standard or Iced Hose, and a choice of your favourite flavour (with or without nicotine). You can choose several flavours for us to bring if you are unsure, we will only charge you for the one you have chosen. You can also choose different types of flavour to swap in the middle of your session if you are planning to smoke for more than 2 hours.

Premium Shisha

Our Premium Shisha consists of Luxury Shisha Equipment from Fumo, a choice of Standard or Iced Hose, and a choice of your favourite flavour (with or without nicotine). Premium Shisha are ideal for group smoking sessions because it has two hoses to smoke from and lasts longer than Classic Shisha. You can choose several flavours for us to bring if you are unsure, we will only charge you for the one you have chosen. You can also choose different types of flavour to swap in the middle of your session if you are planning to smoke for more than 3 hours.

Tropical Shisha

Our Tropical Shisha consists of Luxury Shisha Equipment from Fumo, a choice of Standard of Iced Hose, a choice of your favourite flavour (with or without nicotine) and a choice of your preferred fruit top. A fruit top is an aesthetically pleasing and flavourful substitute to the classic bowl. Tropical Shisha is great for parties and social gatherings! It also lasts longer than Classic Shisha. You can choose several flavours for us to bring if you are unsure, we will only charge you for the one you have chosen. You can also choose different types of flavour to swap in the middle of your session if you are planning to smoke for more than 3 hours.
